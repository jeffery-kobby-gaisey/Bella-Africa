Our Goals

Supporting Women and Girls to Learn, Succeed, and Lead

We serve girls and young women in underprivileged areas in sub-Saharan Africa, tackling the challenges of poverty and gender that limit their opportunities. Our comprehensive approach addresses the root causes of inequality and creates pathways for sustainable change.

Learn

Provide access to quality education and skills training for girls and young women

Succeed

Create opportunities for economic empowerment and career development

Lead

Develop leadership skills and create pathways for women to become community leaders

Our Impact Areas:

Education Healthcare Economic Empowerment Leadership Development Community Building Gender Equality

Strategic Goals 2025-2030

Educational Excellence

Expand our educational programs to reach 10,000 girls across 10 African countries, providing quality education, vocational training, and digital literacy skills.

10,000 Girls Educated
10 Countries
50 Schools Supported

Economic Empowerment

Support 5,000 women entrepreneurs with microfinance, business training, and market access, creating sustainable income opportunities and economic independence.

5,000 Women Entrepreneurs
$2M Microfinance Provided
100 Businesses Created

Healthcare Access

Improve healthcare access for 20,000 women and girls through mobile clinics, health education, and preventive care services in rural communities.

20,000 Women & Girls Served
25 Mobile Clinics
100% Vaccination Coverage

Leadership Development

Develop 1,000 women leaders through leadership training, mentorship programs, and community engagement initiatives, creating a network of empowered change-makers.

1,000 Women Leaders
50 Mentorship Programs
100 Community Projects
