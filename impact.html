Our Journey of Impact

2010

Bella Africa Founded

Dalida Couturier establishes Bella Africa with a vision to empower women and transform communities across Africa.

2015

First Sewing Machine Initiative

Launched our first sewing machine program, providing 25 women with tools and training to start their own businesses.

2018

School Renovation Program

Completed our first school renovation project, improving learning environments for over 100 children.

2020

Healthcare Initiative Launch

Started mobile clinic program, bringing essential healthcare services to remote communities.

2023

Expansion to Multiple Countries

Extended our programs to reach women and communities across 5 African countries.

2025

50 Women Empowered

Celebrated reaching 50 women through our sewing machine initiative, with plans to expand further.
