Our Current Projects

Sewing Machine Initiative

We donated 50 manual sewing machines to 50 young ladies in the central region of Ghana. These women can now create clothing for themselves, their families, and supplement their income.

Completed Central Region, Ghana

Goal: Maintaining the sewing machines and teaching the younger generation is the ultimate goal of Bella Africa.

School Renovation Project

We renovated schools to create supportive learning environments for children in rural communities, ensuring better educational opportunities.

Completed Ghana

Salt Mining

We have established a salt mining and processing facility within the Afiadenyigba community, enabling women to actively engage in the mining and processing of salt—one of the area’s staple and essential food resources.

Completed Ghana
Watch Salt Mining Video

Cassava Processing Machine

We have established a cassava processing facility within a community location, enabling women to produce garri—one of the staple foods widely consumed across West Africa while creating sustainable livelihoods and strengthening local food security.

Completed Western Africa

Impact: This project will provide sustainable income opportunities for local women and support food security.
